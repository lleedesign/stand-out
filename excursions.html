Dunn's River Falls

Put on your swimwear and take the scenic ride to Dunn's River Falls near Ocho Rios.

The swift-flowing waterfalls cascade down more than 600 feet, as the Dunn River rushes from the mountains into the Caribbean. Climb the freshwater falls with the aid of a guide.

Don't forget your water shoes!

Snorkeling in Jamaica

This island nation is one of the most popular vacation destinations in the Caribbean with good reason. It has not only reggae music that creates a special vibe attracting thousands of tourists year after year, but also perfect white sandy beaches with vibrant reef systems that make it a top choice among those who love water sports. The warm, crystal clear sea offers superb snorkeling in Jamaica so you can easily discover the diverse marine life. Let's see now what are the best spots to go on an underwater adventure!

Horseback Ride & Swim

Saddle up for some of the best guided horseback riding in Jamaica. Take a spectacular journey on a well-trained horse, ambling along panoramic cliff-side trails through a tropical rainforest and into the Jamaican countryside. But your ride isn't over until you follow a special path down to the beach for a refreshing jaunt aboard your horse through the warm Caribbean surf.

Canopy Ziplining

Travel high above the Jamaican jungle from a series of decks and platforms. Soar silently above the rainforest. Hear and see the rushing waters below, as you zip across a thousand-foot stretch of river canyon at speeds of up to 35 miles per hour. Your guide – a certified specialist in tropical canopy exploration – will provide tips about what to see and do during this amazing adventure. Let the fun begin!
